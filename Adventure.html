The word ‘adventure’ usually has positive connotations. Though it may be challenging, an adventure usually makes us feel excited and energized. There are many different types of adventure. It might be argued that even something that other people might think is ordinary is an adventure, if it makes us feel challenged and excited. For instance, an adventure might be:
  • Climbing a mountain
  • Learning a new language
  • Traveling to a new country
  • Going to the top of a tall building in the city
  • Going white water rafting

  2. Stan Hayward --> Above all, adventure mean moving into the unknown. It means you are choosing to do, or being forced to do something that you are not sure how to deal with.

    • It might be that you are risking your life and limb.
    • It might be that you risk your livelihood.
    • It might be that you risk your relationships.
    • It might be that you risk your beliefs.
    Whatever, the consequence of taking the risk means that you are wiser about yourself than you were before. If you survive. Assuming you do survive then even if you failed to deal with the situation, you will have learnt the pitfalls of the event itself, and your own shortcomings.
